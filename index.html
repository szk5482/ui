Web standards cross browsing

The web's usefulness and growth depends on its universality.
We should be able to publish regardless of the software we use,
the computer we have, the language we speak, whether we are wired or wireless, regardless of our sensory or interaction modes.
We should be able to access the web from any kind of hardware that can connect to the Internet.

What is Web standards

The term Web Standards is featured prominently all over the web,
in meetings with customers and amongst web developers in general.
However, the problem is that people have different views on what it actually means and encompasses.
Therefore, I will try to explain my views on it and what most people actually mean when they refer to Web Standards.
